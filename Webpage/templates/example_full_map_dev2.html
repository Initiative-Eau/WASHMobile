USING THE INTERFACE:

Access our most up-to-date water quality data for our monitored sources by navigating through the map interface above. Click on individual water points to access collected data for that source.

We currently monitor water quality in the cities of Ouagadougou and Fada N'Gourma, Burkina Faso. Contact us to express interest in expanding the program to a city of interest.
